Why should you visit China?

China is a country full of experiences and 5,000 years of culture, it boasts some of the greatest food cuisines from the sweet Shanghai cuisine to the Sichuan spiciness. China has a wide range of geographical features that you can traverse from Tianshan Mountain Range to the north China plain. It boasts some of the most spectacular and advanced citys to small traditional fishing villages.

Oriental Pearl Tower

The Oriental Pearl Radio & Television Tower is a TV tower in Shanghai. Its location at the tip of Lujiazui in the Pudong New Area by the side of Huangpu River, opposite The Bund, makes it a distinct landmark in the area. Its principal designers were Jiang Huan Chen, Lin Benlin, and Zhang Xiulin. Wiki

1 Century Ave, Lu Jia Zui, Pudong, Shanghai, China
